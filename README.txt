AdSense Compliance Pack — 2025-08-31

Install
-------
1) Upload /assets/adsafe.js to https://sebasuchan2212.github.io/assets/adsafe.js 
2) Use ad-snippet.html on CONTENT PAGES ONLY (NOT verify.html, 404, thank-you or login).
3) Replace data-ad-slot with your own slot IDs.
4) Integrate a Google-certified CMP (Funding Choices, Sourcepoint, CookieYes, etc.).
   Until CMP is present, adsafe.js will request Non-Personalized Ads and pause until ready.
5) Link privacy pages (privacy.html, privacy-en.html, privacy-es.html) in footer & hreflang.

Policy focus
------------
- No ads on screens without publisher content or under construction.
- Keep ads away from interactive controls; no deceptive placements.
- EEA/UK/CH: use a Certified CMP (per Google policy).

Request a review
----------------
AdSense → Policy center → Fix → Request review.
